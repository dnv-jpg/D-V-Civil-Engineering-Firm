Who We Are

D & V Civil Engineering is my fictional engineering management firm for this course, led by Dayannara Vilcarino. The company focuses on modern project management practices: stakeholder integration, risk and schedule control, quality management, and sustainable infrastructure planning. Our approach aligns with the IE492 syllabus and real-world engineering management principles from the course resources (Kerzner, Ranky videos, and multimedia library).

Student / Owner: Dayannara Vilcarino

Course: IE492 Engineering Management (Spring 2026)

Instructor: Dr. Ranky

Management Approach

We apply integrated, real-world engineering management: no isolated systems, clear project lifecycles, and emphasis on avoiding project failures through best practices in staffing, change management, closeout, and documentation. Our methods are informed by the course videos, Kerzner PM text, and the Ranky multimedia resources.
