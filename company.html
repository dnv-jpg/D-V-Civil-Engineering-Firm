D-V Civil Engineering Firm

Modern Engineering Management | Quality + Project Delivery

Company Overview

D-V Civil Engineering Firm is a fictional company created for IE492. The focus is delivering infrastructure and construction projects using modern engineering management principles: scope control, quality management, scheduling, cost tracking, risk management, and continuous improvement.

Mission: Deliver safe, high-quality civil projects on time and on budget using modern project and quality management systems.

Core Focus: Real-world engineering management (visual management, prevention of defects, process improvement, and stakeholder communication).

Services

  • Project planning & scheduling (milestones, deliverables, tracking)
  • Cost estimating & budget monitoring
  • Quality management (inspection points, defect prevention, lessons learned)
  • Risk & safety management (risk register, mitigation planning)
  • Client communication & documentation systems

Management Approach

  • Systems thinking: projects are integrated—people + process + technology
  • Visual management: dashboards, progress tracking, and clear reporting
  • Continuous improvement: analyze issues, prevent recurrence, improve the process
